About Me

With over 15 years of experience in environmental research, climate change adaptation, and sustainability science, I am deeply committed to advancing knowledge and policies that promote resilient ecosystems and sustainable communities.

As a Senior Research Fellow at the Institute for Environment and Sanitation Studies (IESS), University of Ghana, my work bridges scientific research, policy engagement, and capacity building to drive impactful solutions for environmental sustainability.

I actively engage with policymakers, local communities, and development agencies to co-create strategies for sustainable environmental management, with a focus on climate resilience and community-based adaptation.
